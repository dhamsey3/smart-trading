ABOUT US

"WELCOME TO MY CLIMATE DEFINITION"

Hi! I'm Patricia Korir — an environmentalist with a background in environmental studies and international development. I've spent years working on climate-related issues, but it wasn't until I took a step back and truly reflected that I realized how personal this journey has to be.

My Climate Definition was born from the idea that understanding climate change isn't just about the science — it's about the stories we share, the experiences we go through, and the changes we make in our own lives. I started this blog with the aim of breaking down the complexities of climate change and personalizing it for all of us. It's about making it more relatable, engaging, and ultimately inspiring action.

"THE LEARNING JOURNEY"

I've come to realize that climate change isn't something you just learn once. It's a continuous journey of learning, relearning, and unlearning. From discovering the basic science behind climate change to unlearning old habits and ideas that no longer serve us, this process has shaped my understanding and deepened my commitment to change.

This space is where I share my own story — and the stories of many others — to show that we are all on this journey together. This platform is about the planet and how we, as individuals and communities, adapt, rethink, and act. Every step we take in this journey matters, and through this blog, I hope to inspire positive change in you, just as I'm continually inspired by the incredible transformations I've witnessed.

OUR MISSION & VISION

MISSION

To personalize climate change by sharing stories, experiences, and insights that simplify its complexities, educate and raise awareness, inspire positive action, and empower individuals to adapt, embrace sustainable lifestyles, and drive meaningful social change.

VISION

A world where everyone is informed, empowered to embrace change, and inspired to shape a sustainable and equitable future for all.
